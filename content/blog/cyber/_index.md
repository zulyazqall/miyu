**Cybersecurity** is how that information is kept **safe**.
There is a lot of information being sent online with various devices like phones, computers, smart appliances to name a few.


## Beware of Cybercriminals!
Sometimes cybercriminals can take control of devices and steal important information or infect it with a digital virus.
