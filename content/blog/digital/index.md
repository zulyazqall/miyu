A **Data** is a collection of unorganized words, numbers and images.

**Information** is the meaningful and organaized form of data.

```
The main works of computer is accept **data** from you and convert the data into **information**. Finally, the information will be stored in its **Memory**.
```

## Example
You can remember your name, address, contact numbers etc because the information stored in the memory (**Brain**)
